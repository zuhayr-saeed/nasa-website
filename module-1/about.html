About NASA

This era is a remarkable era for NASA, characterized by numerous milestones in space exploration and advancements in technology. During this decade, NASA saw the launch and deployment of the Hubble Space Telescope, a pivotal moment that revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos. The telescope provided breathtaking images of distant galaxies, nebulae, and other celestial objects.

Another significant achievement was the initiation of the Space Shuttle program, which enabled regular manned spaceflights. The program saw the launch of iconic missions like the repair of the Hubble Space Telescope, which extended its scientific capabilities.

The era also marked a turning point in international collaboration with the construction of the International Space Station (ISS). NASA played a key role in this multinational effort, fostering international partnerships to build and operate the ISS.
